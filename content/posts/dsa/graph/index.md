## What is a Graph?

A graph G is an ordered pair of a set V of vertices and a set of E of edges.

G can be defines as follows

$$G=\left(V,E\right)$$

ordered pair:
$$(a, b) \neq \left(b,a\right) \text{where} \,\, a \neq b$$g
